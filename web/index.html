Career Scouting Report

ScoutCard

Turn your work history into a collectible stat card.

✓ Nothing is uploaded — parsed on your device, gone when you close the tab.

  1. On LinkedIn: Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data. Request at least Profile, Positions, Skills, Connections, Recommendations, Endorsements, Shares, Comments.
  2. Download the .zip LinkedIn emails you.
  3. Drop it below. Richer stats (connections, endorsements, activity) — only works for your own account.
  1. Open any public LinkedIn profile you can view (yours or someone else's) → click "More" → "Save to PDF".
  2. Drop the downloaded .pdf below. Thinner stats (no connections/endorsements/activity — only career history, skills, certs, languages, education) but works on anyone's public profile.

Tap the card to see what the score means
